Caveats

Gross revenue (DEF-004) counts completed + refunded orders (DEF-003); net revenue (DEF-005) subtracts refund value dated by return_date for this full-window total (dating policy is moot at full-window grain — see DEF-005 caveat). Revenue by loyalty tier and by store attributes each order to the canonical customer (DEF-014) — absorbed near-dupe customers' orders count under their original's tier/identity, never split. The 2020 COVID dip and summer-seasonality shading on the trend chart are visual annotations over the captured Q02 series, not separate query outputs.
