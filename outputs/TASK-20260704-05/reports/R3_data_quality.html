Caveats

Per-D-id totals in the census bar are the sum of that D-id's own captured sub-pattern rows from B04 (e.g. D1 = 240+180+240+360 = 1,020) — every addend is visible in the detail table below the chart and in the hover tooltip, so the rollup is fully auditable against medallion/a_bronze/EXPECTED_OUTPUTS.md. D12/D14/D20 are distinct-value counts (controlled-vocabulary variety), not row counts, and are labeled as such. "Total rows profiled" is the sum of the 14 B01 per-table row counts (also shown in full below) — a rollup of already-aggregated captured numbers, not a re-aggregation of any fact table. DEF-014's near-dupe resolution finding (rule-3 email+birth-date fallback resolves zero candidates) is a live-data result, not a bug — the generator mutated candidate emails so no fallback match exists; the branch stays live in the view for rule fidelity (DEF-014 caveat).
