Notes & lineage

Caveats

Unit margin (DEF-021) uses current product cost — there is no historical cost table, so margins on older orders are computed against today's cost basis (a stated limitation, not a defect). Median unit margin (Q14) is computed over the 151,818 DEF-003 revenue lines in fact_order_lines; with an even line count the median is the rounded mean of the two middle sorted values. Below-cost lines (realized, 3,444) and below-cost catalog products (D16, 17) are RULE-008 planted features — surfaced here deliberately, never filtered out of any figure on this page.

Known data gaps (escalated to sql-builder, not silently worked around)

  • Q07 grain mismatch — R2's monthly top-5-category trend needs 450 row-level rows; only an aggregate signature was captured. See escalation box under "Primary view" above.
  • Q09 grain mismatch — R2's price-vs-cost scatter needs 850 per-product rows; only an aggregate signature was captured. See escalation box under "Price-vs-cost anomalies" above.
