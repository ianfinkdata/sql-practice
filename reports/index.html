Oakhaven Medallion Project — Exploratory Reports

Self-contained HTML reports built on a bronze → silver → gold medallion pipeline over the Oakhaven Outfitters practice dataset. Every number on every chart traces to a captured, reproducible gold or bronze query — see each report's lineage footer.

R1

Sales Explorer

Revenue trend by channel, seasonality and the 2020 dip, revenue by store and loyalty tier, promo share.

 R2

Product & Category Explorer

Category performance, margin distribution, price-vs-cost anomalies, top/bottom products.

 R3

Data Quality Explorer

The bronze-to-silver dirt census, near-dupe resolution, sentinel values, and planted anomalies — proof the cleaning rules did what they say.
Structural template for building new reports: template.html. Source and pipeline: github.com/ianfinkdata/sql-practice.
